RECITALS

A. The Steenkamp Family Trust ("the Trust") was established by Trust Deed dated 14 March 2019, registered with the Master of the High Court, Windhoek, under Master's reference W.M. 4471/2019.

B. The Trust Deed in clause 2.2 authorises the trustees to accept further assets for the benefit of the Trust and, in clause 5, confers full and unfettered discretion on the trustees to determine the beneficiaries and amounts of distributions.

C. The original Trust Deed listed the beneficiaries as (i) Ms. Wilhelmina Petronella Steenkamp, (ii) Christiaan Hendrik Steenkamp, (iii) Annaline Maré Steenkamp, and (iv) any further descendants of the Founder born after the date of the Trust Deed.

D. The Founder's son Christiaan Hendrik Steenkamp and his wife, Ms. Lerato Nomvula Steenkamp (née Dlamini), have on 4 July 2023 been blessed with the birth of a child, Mr. Elias Christiaan Steenkamp (DOB: 4 July 2023, ID to be assigned), who is a descendant of the Founder and therefore already qualifies as a beneficiary under clause C(iv) above.

E. In addition, the Founder wishes to formally add his daughter-in-law, Ms. Lerato Nomvula Steenkamp (née Dlamini) (Namibian ID: 890217 0024 311), as a named beneficiary of the Trust, as she does not qualify under the existing "descendants" category.

F. All trustees have consented to the addition of Ms. Lerato Nomvula Steenkamp as a named beneficiary by written resolution dated 5 September 2023 (attached as Annexure A).

G. The trustees have obtained the approval of the Master of the High Court, Windhoek, for this amendment by letter dated 8 September 2023 (attached as Annexure B).

---

NOW THEREFORE the trustees hereby amend the Trust Deed as follows:

1. AMENDMENT TO CLAUSE 2 — BENEFICIARIES

    Clause 2 (Beneficiaries) of the Trust Deed is hereby amended to read as follows:

    "2. BENEFICIARIES

    The beneficiaries of the Trust shall be:
    (a) Ms. Wilhelmina Petronella Steenkamp (ID: 720803 0023 182), spouse of the Founder;
    (b) Christiaan Hendrik Steenkamp (ID: 021215 5015 088), son of the Founder;
    (c) Annaline Maré Steenkamp (ID: 050603 0014 079), daughter of the Founder;
    (d) Ms. Lerato Nomvula Steenkamp (née Dlamini) (Namibian ID: 890217 0024 311), daughter-in-law of the Founder; and
    (e) Any further descendants of the Founder born after the date of the original Trust Deed, including but not limited to Elias Christiaan Steenkamp (DOB: 4 July 2023)."

2. CONDITIONS APPLICABLE TO NEW BENEFICIARY

    2.1 Ms. Lerato Nomvula Steenkamp shall be a discretionary beneficiary only. She shall have no vested right to any distribution from the Trust.

    2.2 The trustees shall, in exercising their discretion to make distributions to Ms. Lerato Nomvula Steenkamp, give due consideration to her personal circumstances, financial needs, and the overall needs of the other beneficiaries.

    2.3 In the event that the marriage between Ms. Lerato Nomvula Steenkamp and Christiaan Hendrik Steenkamp is dissolved by divorce or annulment, the trustees shall give particular consideration to whether continued distributions to Ms. Steenkamp remain in the best interests of the Trust and its other beneficiaries. For the avoidance of doubt, such dissolution shall not automatically disqualify Ms. Steenkamp as a beneficiary; such decision rests solely with the trustees.

3. ACKNOWLEDGEMENT OF ELIAS CHRISTIAAN STEENKAMP

    3.1 The trustees hereby confirm that Elias Christiaan Steenkamp (DOB: 4 July 2023), as a descendant of the Founder, qualifies as a beneficiary pursuant to clause (e) of the amended Beneficiaries clause above.

    3.2 Distributions for the benefit of Elias Christiaan Steenkamp shall, until he attains the age of 21 years, be administered by his parents jointly as natural guardians, or as otherwise directed by the trustees.

4. CONFIRMATION OF EXISTING TERMS

    Save as expressly amended herein, all other terms and conditions of the Trust Deed dated 14 March 2019 remain in full force and effect.

5. REGISTRATION

    The trustees undertake to register this Deed of Amendment with the Master of the High Court, Windhoek, within 30 days of execution hereof in accordance with the requirements of the Trustee Act (Act No. 57 of 1951).
