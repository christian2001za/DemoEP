PREAMBLE

The undersigned, being all the directors of VdB African Holdings (Pty) Ltd ("the Company"), hereby pass the following resolutions in writing in lieu of a meeting of the board of directors, pursuant to Article 12.4 of the Company's Memorandum of Incorporation and Section 74 of the Companies Act (Act No. 28 of 2004), read with Section 60 of the Companies Act.

---

BACKGROUND

A. The Company was incorporated on 19 January 2024 as a wholly owned subsidiary of Van den Berg Holdings B.V. (Netherlands), pursuant to the board resolution of Van den Berg Holdings B.V. dated 22 November 2023 (Reference: EP/NL/MIN/2023/119).

B. Upon incorporation, Mr. Jean-Pierre van Niekerk of Edgepoint Trust (Namibia) (Pty) Ltd was appointed as the sole initial director and resident agent of the Company pending the appointment of permanent directors.

C. Van den Berg Holdings B.V. now wishes to appoint two (2) permanent directors to the board of the Company, with Mr. van Niekerk remaining as independent director and resident representative.

D. The proposed new directors are:
    1. Mr. Pieter Adriaan van den Berg (Dutch national, Passport No. NV7841023), CEO of Van den Berg Holdings B.V.;
    2. Ms. Elise Jacoba van den Berg-Lourens (Dutch national, Passport No. NV6602917), CFO of Van den Berg Holdings B.V.

E. The Memorandum of Incorporation of the Company requires that the Company have at least one director who is ordinarily resident in Namibia. Mr. van Niekerk satisfies this requirement.

---

RESOLUTIONS

RESOLUTION 1: APPOINTMENT OF MR. PIETER ADRIAAN VAN DEN BERG AS DIRECTOR

IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED that Mr. Pieter Adriaan van den Berg (born 17 April 1968, Dutch national, Passport No. NV7841023, residing at Herengracht 412, 1017 BX Amsterdam, the Netherlands) be and is hereby appointed as an executive director of the Company with effect from 15 March 2024.

Mr. van den Berg's appointment is subject to:
(a) Receipt of a duly completed and signed consent to act as director (CM27B form or equivalent under the Namibian Companies Act);
(b) Confirmation that he is not disqualified from acting as a director under Section 69 of the Companies Act.

Mr. van den Berg shall serve as Co-Chief Executive Officer of the Company.

---

RESOLUTION 2: APPOINTMENT OF MS. ELISE JACOBA VAN DEN BERG-LOURENS AS DIRECTOR

IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED that Ms. Elise Jacoba van den Berg-Lourens (born 3 August 1972, Dutch national, Passport No. NV6602917, residing at Herengracht 412, 1017 BX Amsterdam, the Netherlands) be and is hereby appointed as a non-executive director of the Company with effect from 15 March 2024.

Ms. van den Berg-Lourens's appointment is subject to the same conditions as set out in Resolution 1 mutatis mutandis.

Ms. van den Berg-Lourens shall serve as Financial Oversight Director of the Company.

---

RESOLUTION 3: CONFIRMATION OF MR. JEAN-PIERRE VAN NIEKERK AS INDEPENDENT DIRECTOR AND RESIDENT REPRESENTATIVE

IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED that the appointment of Mr. Jean-Pierre van Niekerk (Namibian ID: 750622 5015 099), as Independent Director and Resident Representative of the Company, is confirmed and ratified with effect from the date of incorporation of the Company (19 January 2024).

Mr. van Niekerk shall be remunerated for his directorship and resident representative services in accordance with the fee schedule of Edgepoint Trust (Namibia) (Pty) Ltd, as amended from time to time.

---

RESOLUTION 4: DELEGATION OF AUTHORITY

IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED that:

(a) The Board of Directors collectively shall have authority to bind the Company for transactions exceeding NAD 500,000 in value, requiring at least two director signatures;

(b) For day-to-day operational transactions up to NAD 500,000, Mr. van Niekerk, acting in his capacity as resident representative and independent director, is authorised to act on behalf of the Company without requiring further co-signature;

(c) For transactions exceeding NAD 2,000,000, unanimous board approval shall be required by written resolution of all directors;

(d) The Company's banking relationship with First National Bank Namibia (FNB), Account No. 62 8841 9900 (Business Account, Windhoek Main Branch), is hereby confirmed, and the above signing authorities are communicated to FNB accordingly.

---

RESOLUTION 5: NOTIFICATION TO BIPA

IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED that the Company Secretary (currently Edgepoint Trust (Namibia) (Pty) Ltd) is authorised and directed to:
(a) File the requisite notification of director changes with the Business and Intellectual Property Authority (BIPA) within 14 days of the date of this resolution, in accordance with Section 87 of the Companies Act;
(b) Update the Company's register of directors accordingly.
